Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 1, 2026

Lago Apps Group ("we", "our", or "us") operates mobile applications including Lago Mind and any future applications published under the Lago brand. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your information.

Information We Collect

Our apps are designed with privacy in mind. Depending on how you use our applications, we may process the following types of information:

  • Data you enter directly into the app (such as financial records, notes, tasks, goals, or other personal content).
  • Basic device information such as operating system version and app version (only if required for app functionality or troubleshooting).

In general, Lago Apps Group applications are designed so that your personal data is stored locally on your device.

How We Use Information

We use information only to:

  • Provide core app functionality
  • Improve app performance and stability
  • Fix bugs and technical issues
  • Support future feature development

Data Storage

Unless explicitly stated otherwise, all user data is stored locally on your device. We do not operate cloud storage or user accounts for our applications.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

We do not share your personal data with third parties because we do not collect or store it on external servers.

Future Features

Lago Apps Group may introduce optional features in the future such as cloud synchronization, analytics, crash reporting, or subscription services. If such features are introduced, this Privacy Policy will be updated before they are enabled.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data. However, since most data is stored locally on your device, security also depends on your device protection.

Children’s Privacy

Our applications are not intended for children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Third-Party Services

Our apps currently do not rely on third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Changes to This Policy
